Reminder:
• If the Works Cited begins with U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics, your in-text citation uses a short title (because it’s a group author and the title is clearer).
• If the Works Cited begins with CareerOneStop, the in-text citation uses CareerOneStop (or the short title).
• These sources don’t have page numbers, so your in-text citations will not use a page.
Examples of WRONG citations (what mismatch looks like)

• If your Works Cited says CareerOneStop but your in-text says (BLS), that’s a mismatch.
• If your Works Cited has “Dental Hygienists” but your in-text says (“EMT Pathway”), that’s a mismatch.
